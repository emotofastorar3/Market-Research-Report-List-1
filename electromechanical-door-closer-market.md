<p><p>The electromechanical door closer market is categorized into two main types: single-point and multi-point electromechanical door closers. Single-point door closers operate at a single location, providing efficient closure for standard doors. These are ideal for residential and light commercial use. In contrast, multi-point door closers utilize mechanisms that engage multiple locking points along the door frame, enhancing security and sealing. This type is commonly found in larger commercial spaces or external doors, offering superior durability and stability.</p></p>
