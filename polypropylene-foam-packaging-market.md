<p><strong>The Polypropylene Foam Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expanded Polypropylene Foam</li><li>Extruded Polypropylene Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene foam packaging market is primarily divided into two types: expanded polypropylene foam (EPP) and extruded polypropylene foam (XPP). Expanded polypropylene foam is created through a process that expands polypropylene beads, resulting in a lightweight, shock-absorbing material often used for protective packaging and automotive applications. In contrast, extruded polypropylene foam is formed through a continuous extrusion process, producing a denser and more uniform structure, making it suitable for insulation, automotive interiors, and various industrial applications.</p></p>
