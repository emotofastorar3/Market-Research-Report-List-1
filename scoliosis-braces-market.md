<p><p>The scoliosis braces market comprises three main types: cast braces, rigid braces, and non-rigid dynamic braces. Cast braces provide immobilization and support, often used for severe cases during initial treatment. Rigid braces offer significant correction through structured designs, typically utilized in adolescents to manage curve progression. Non-rigid dynamic braces are flexible and adjustable, allowing for gradual correction while promoting mobility. Each type serves distinct needs based on the severity and age of the patient, ultimately aiming to improve spinal alignment and posture.</p></p>
